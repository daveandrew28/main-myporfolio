Educational Tour November 19-22, 2024

Educational Tour Journal: Cebu & Bohol Experience

Dave Andrew Serafin

UI/UX Developer & BSIT Student

Introduction

Our educational tour to Cebu and Bohol from November 19-22, 2024, was an incredible journey of learning and discovery. As BSIT students, we had the opportunity to visit leading tech companies, experience innovative technologies, and explore the beautiful islands of Cebu and Bohol.

Tour Highlights

  • Company visits to 5 tech companies
  • Educational sessions with industry experts
  • Cultural and historical exploration
  • Team building and networking

Tour Timeline

Day 1

November 19, Wednesday - Arrival in Cebu

Our adventure began with an early morning flight from Davao to Cebu. After arriving at Mactan Cebu International Airport, we were greeted by our tour guide and proceeded with our exciting itinerary.

8:45 a.m. Departure from Davao to Cebu via Philippine Airlines
9:50 a.m. Arrival in Mactan Cebu International Airport
11:00 a.m. Lunch Buffet at Somac Korean Restaurant
1:30 p.m. Visit to WORLDTECH INFORMATION SOLUTIONS, INC.
4:30 p.m. Check-in at Bai Hotel
Day 2

November 20, Thursday - Company Visits in Cebu

The second day was filled with exciting company visits to leading IT companies in Cebu. We explored CODECHUM and RIVAN IT CEBU, learning about the thriving tech industry and innovation happening in the region.

CODECHUM

An educational technology platform designed to make learning programming accessible and engaging for students and teachers.

RIVAN IT CEBU

A training center specializing in IT and networking certification courses such as Cisco CCNA/CCNP, Linux, security, and VoIP.

Day 3

November 21, Friday - Travel to Bohol

We bid farewell to Cebu and embarked on an exciting journey to Bohol. After visiting MATA TECHNOLOGIES, we took a scenic ferry ride to Tagbilaran City and proceeded to the beautiful island of Panglao.

MATA TECHNOLOGIES, INC.

Mata Technologies Inc. is a homegrown provider of virtual tours for real estate in the Philippines. It is also the virtual reality map provider of tourist destinations in the country.

Day 4

November 22, Saturday - Bohol Exploration

Our final day was an incredible adventure exploring the natural wonders and cultural treasures of Bohol. From the mysterious Chocolate Hills to the adorable Tarsiers, and ending with a delicious dinner before heading home.

Tour Highlights:

T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117 Emergency Response Center Loboc River Cruise Chocolate Hills Baclayon Church Man-Made Forest

Company Visits & Learnings

MATA Technologies, Inc.

Virtual Reality Real Estate Tourism

Observation/Learnings: Visiting MATA Technologies was an eye-opening experience that showcased the provided map of Mata Technologies is the premier provider of 360° virtual Tours for real estate and tourism destinations in the Philippines. During our visit, we had the opportunity to explore their innovative virtual tour technology, which allows users to experience properties and tourist spots in an immersive way. The team at MATA Technologies demonstrated how their platform works, highlighting its user-friendly interface and high-quality visuals. We also learned about the company's mission to revolutionize the way people view real estate and travel destinations through cutting-edge technology. Overall, the visit was both educational and inspiring, giving us a glimpse into the future of virtual experiences.

Codechum

Education Technology Programming Learning Platform

Observation/Learnings: My visit to CodeChum at CIT (Cebu Institute of Technology–University) was very insightful. The session was facilitated by the Chief Operating Officer, Xavier David Maranga, who introduced us to CodeChum and shared how the company started and grew. He talked about their journey, the challenges they faced, and how they worked hard to achieve what CodeChum is today. He also shared his personal story of learning how to code, including the inspiring experiences he had with his brothers, the creators of CodeChum.

Photos from Codechum Visit:

Wordtech Information Solutions, Inc.

Cybersecurity IT Consulting Training

Observation/Learnings: The visit to Wordtech Information Solutions was an incredible learning opportunity. The session was expertly facilitated by Mrs. Elizabeth Arquiza. Attendees gained a clear understanding of the company's focus: providing cybersecurity solutions for Operational Technology (OT) environments. Their critical mission is to help secure the Industrial Internet (IIoT). In addition to their core business, the session included valuable information about the fundamental mechanisms of how the internet works.

Photos from Wordtech Visit:

RIVAN IT CEBU

IT Training Networking Certification

Observation/Learnings: My visit to Rivan IT in Cebu was a very educational and enjoyable experience. During the visit, we met our facilitator, who introduced us to the company, which specializes in IT training in Cebu. As part of our learning, the facilitator taught us about telephones and how to use the CLI (Command Line Interface) to connect them in order to make calls or ring. Another facilitator then introduced us to Layer 1 concepts, such as routers, switches, and cables, and showed us how to properly connect these devices. Overall, the experience was both fun and entertaining, and I learned a lot about IT systems and practical networking skills.

Photos from RIVAN IT Visit:

T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117

Government Disaster Risk Management Security

Observation/Learnings: My Visit to T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117 was an eye-opening experience. We were able to see whats inside especially the operations center where they monitor and respond to emergencies in Bohol. The staff explained how they use technology to track weather patterns, coordinate rescue operations, and communicate with other agencies. I was impressed by their dedication and professionalism in handling disaster situations. They also showed us some of the equipment they use, such as radios, computers, and mapping software. Overall, the visit highlighted the importance of disaster preparedness and the role of technology in saving lives.

Accommodations

Bai Hotel

Cebu City, Philippines

bai Hotel Cebu is a 4-star hospitality property located in Mandaue City, Cebu. Opened last 2017, bai Hotel Cebu is an independent hotel. Nestled between the bustling cities of Mandaue and Cebu, it presents spectacular harbor and city views with ultra-modern amenities and facilities. A contemporary chic business hotel and culinary destination in one of the country's top business and leisure destinations. All 668 guestrooms and 8 restaurants are stylishly designed in contemporary hues that evoke an ambiance of modern luxury and spaciousness secured with a world-class access control system. Envisioned to enhance the beauty and transformation of the highly-urbanized cities of Mandaue and Cebu, bai Hotel Cebu is for the business and leisure traveler who seeks boldness, authenticity, and innovativeness for each visit, whether it is for the first time or succeeding visits. A member of the renowned Worldhotels, bai Hotel Cebu is Your Host in the South that links the traveler to a global commitment to enhanced safety, comfortable accommodations, and distinctly bai service in inspiring and memorable settings.

Panglao Vista Suites

Panglao, Bohol, Philippines

Panglao Vista Suites (also known as Panglao Oceanview Resort by SMS Hospitality) is a newly opened sanctuary in Panglao, Bohol, offering luxury, tranquility, and stunning natural beauty with modern amenities like free WiFi, an outdoor pool, 24/7 front desk, and airport shuttle service, aiming to provide memorable stays for romantic getaways, family fun, or peaceful retreats.
